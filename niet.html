Welcome to Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular battle royale game developed by Epic Games. The game is free-to-play and is available on multiple platforms including PC, console, and mobile devices.

Gameplay

Fortnite is a battle royale game where 100 players fight to be the last one standing. Players can gather resources and build structures to help them survive. The game also features various weapons and items that can be used to gain an advantage over opponents.

About Fortnite

Fortnite was first released in 2017 and quickly gained a massive following. The game has since become one of the most popular battle royale games in the world, with millions of players logging in to play each day.
